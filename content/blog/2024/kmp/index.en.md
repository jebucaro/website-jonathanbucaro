🎓 I’ve earned the Kanban Management Professional (KMP) certification from Kanban University! 🚀

To achieve this certification, I completed two key courses:

1. **Kanban System Design (KSD)**: This course provided me with the fundamentals of the Kanban Method. I learned how to design effective Kanban systems using the STATIK approach, improving team visibility and collaboration. Additionally, I understood how “Pull” systems help reduce overload and how metrics like Lead Time and Cumulative Flow Diagrams support continuous improvement.
2. **Kanban Systems Improvement (KSI)**: In this course, I gained the ability to scale connected systems and optimize them through evolutionary changes. I learned how to identify and manage delays, variability, and bottlenecks in workflows, using key metrics to drive improvements.

This certification marks a significant step forward in my professional development, enabling me to implement more effective agile practices and enhance project management. I’m excited to apply this knowledge to optimize workflow and boost my team’s efficiency. 💪
